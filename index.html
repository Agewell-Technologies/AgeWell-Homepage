Connect by AgeWell

Connect by AgeWell is an online service creating meaningful connections across generations to foster wellness and positive aging by connecting members with virtual companions, providing a space to participate in events from the comfort of your home or facility and facilitaing a platform for members and thier caregivers to stay organinzed.
